FAQS

Do students need to sign up before using the system?

No, student just need to fill in the form provided in the system.

Who will check the student booking application?

Admin or KUPTM staff will check booking's form send by students. They also will approve the form.

What the best time to book the court?

At least 12 hours before the time using the court.

What happen if booking appication is not accepted?

Student will received the notification or student can meet the staff directly at front table.

What day the court is not available for student?

The day when KUPTM organize a program.
